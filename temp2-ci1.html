Template 2 — Compound Interest
Problem: A sum of ₹5000 amounts to ₹6050 in 2 years at compound interest. Find the annual rate.
P = ₹5,000
→
A = ₹6,050
→
t = 2 years
Formula
→
A = P (1 + r)^t
Substitute
→
6050 = 5000 (1 + r)^2
Divide
→
(1 + r)^2 = 6050 / 5000 = 1.21
Square root
→
1 + r = √1.21 = 1.1
Subtract 1
→
r = 1.1 - 1 = 0.1 = 10%
Answer: Rate = 10% per annum
